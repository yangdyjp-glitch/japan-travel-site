FAQ

Questions Before You Reach Out

Are flights included?

International flights can be quoted separately. Trip pricing is structured around land arrangements, local transportation, accommodations, guides, activities, and selected meals.

What ages are suitable?

Anime, snow, and Halloween trips are especially strong for teens and families. Craft and art trips can also work well for schools, adults, and custom groups.

Can schools customize the itinerary?

Yes. Each trip can be customized with pre-trip briefings, reflection sessions, institutional visits, or theme-specific activities.

When should seasonal trips be planned?

Ski trips, Halloween dates, Ghibli Park, USJ, Naoshima, Teshima, and other reservation-heavy experiences are best planned 6–9 months in advance.
