# Image Brief / 画像ブリーフ

The site image positions have been mapped to approved Fig. X / 図X visual assets. Use this document as a reference when replacing or refining photography in the future.

## General Direction / 全体方針

- Use real photography whenever possible.
- Prefer images with people actively experiencing Japan, not only empty scenery.
- Updated visual direction: brighter, more urban, and sky-blue led. Prioritize clear daylight city energy, blue skies, clean modern streets, Tokyo skyline, Tokyo Tower, Shibuya/Shinjuku city scenes, and vivid but polished color.
- Keep the tone lively and premium; avoid dull beige travel imagery, overly dark night imagery, generic empty scenery, or overly quiet editorial photos.
- For student and family products, show safety, warmth, group scale, and guided activity.
- For premium art and culture products, use calmer composition, architectural detail, landscape, and museum-adjacent atmosphere.
- All images should have usage rights cleared for commercial website use.

## Replacement Notes / 差し替えメモ

- Keep hero images landscape-oriented, ideally 16:9 or wider.
- Keep card images consistent in crop ratio, ideally 4:3 or 3:2.
- Gallery images can mix wide and detail shots, but the website should crop them consistently.
- Keep descriptive `alt` text when replacing or refining image files.
